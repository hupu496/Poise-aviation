About Director

Ikneet Kaur


Ms. Ikneet Kaur is a distinguished and vigorous Aviation professional with over 11 years of rich experience as an International Cabin Crew, who has successfully flown 10,000 hours on world-class Airbus-330/321/320/319 and Boeing -777LR/777ER/787(Dreamliner) Having worked as an exclusive premium class Cabin Crew in Kingfisher Airlines and Air India, She has traveled extensively throughout the world and has a natural flair to interact with people of different ethnicity with manifold experiences to share.


She maintains an extremely good relationship with Aviation Industry and is known for conducting innumerable training sessions on Grooming, Hospitality, Business and Social etiquettes, behavioral skills, Personality Development and interview skills.


She has also executed many motivational sessions in various institutes, which when weaved together had helped many seekers to portray a positive image and shine out from the crowd. Nothing makes her happier than to help others discover and explore the delights of new experiences and places with her. She maintains an extremely good rapport with her students across various backgrounds and has helped them achieve next level goals and create a name in the industry.
